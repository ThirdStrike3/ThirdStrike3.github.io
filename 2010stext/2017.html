Brock Lesnar vs. Goldberg

WWE WrestleMania 33: The Ultimate Thrill Ride

WWE Universal Title Match

2/4/2017

Redemption after their stinker of a match at Wrestlemania XX. Short and to the point, like matches between two monsters should be. Goldberg's entrance here is pretty lame compared to his old ones though. The match literally goes like this:

German > German > German > Spear > Spear > Spear into the barricade > Spear > Jackhammer > failed Spear (insane leapfrog by Brock) > German > German > German > German > German > German > German > F5

and it works perfectly for what they were trying to accomplish. Both men take some insane punishment in a short amount of time and both look very strong. Maybe I'm just biased because I love German suplexes though.

Rating: 3.5

Recommended to see two big beasts beating chunks out of each other.
