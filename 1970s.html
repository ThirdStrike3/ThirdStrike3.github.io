Bruno Sammartino vs. Ernie Ladd

WWWF on HBO

WWWF Heavyweight Title Match

1/3/1976

Holy fuck, Ernie Ladd is huge. He completely dwarfs Sammartino, who already looks big compared to the ref. It's astounding. He's surprisingly nimble in-ring too, obviously not as quick as some wrestlers but he's a very competent big man especially at selling, and his leg drop looks vicious. I also love the crunch sound the ring makes whenever there's a bump. Ernie's missed top rope splash finish of the match is a bit anticlimactic though. This is a very good match.

Rating: 3.75

Recommended to people looking to get into pre-Golden Era wrestling.
