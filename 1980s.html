Magnum TA vs. Tully Blanchard

NWA Starrcade 1985: The Gathering

NWA United States Heavyweight Title I Quit Steel Cage Match

28/11/1985

So fucking violent. Less a wrestling match and more two men beating the absolute piss out of each other. Those screams into the mic are legitimately terrifying. The image of Magnum piercing Blanchard's forehead with the broken chair leg is one of the most viscerally brilliant things I've ever seen.

Rating: 5

Recommended to fans of hardcore wrestling and its history.
